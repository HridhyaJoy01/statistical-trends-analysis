# World Bank Climate & Energy Statistical Analysis

Statistical analysis and visualisation of World Bank climate and energy
indicators across multiple countries.

## What This Project Does
- Analyses key indicators: Renewable energy consumption, GDP growth (annual %),
  Electric power consumption, and Fossil fuel energy consumption
- Descriptive statistics: mean, median, standard deviation
- Correlation analysis across all indicators
- Visualisations: line charts, bar charts, pie chart, correlation heatmap

## Tech Stack
Python · Pandas · Matplotlib · Seaborn

## Dataset
World Bank Open Data — climate and energy indicators
